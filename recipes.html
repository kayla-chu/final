Pumpkin Pie

Rich and creamy pumpkin pie perfect for fall gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 1 pie crust
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs
  • Spices: cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Mix pumpkin, eggs, milk, and spices.
  3. Pour into crust.
  4. Bake 15 minutes, then reduce heat to 350°F and bake 40–50 minutes.
  5. Cool before serving.

Stir-Fried Udon

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles
  • Cabbage, carrots, mushrooms
  • Soy sauce & oyster sauce
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles until soft.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables.
  3. Add noodles and sauces.
  4. Toss until coated.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes & onions
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani spices

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook rice separately.
  3. Layer rice and chicken.
  4. Steam until fragrant.
